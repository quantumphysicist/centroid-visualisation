Centroid Visualiser

The centroid of a finite set of points is the (unweighted) arithmetic mean of their coordinates — equivalently, the centre of mass if each point is assigned equal mass.

For n points it is calculated as cx = 1/n ∑ xi, cy = 1/n ∑ yi.

Click inside the canvas to add points and watch the centroid update in real time.
